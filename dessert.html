Dessert

Dessert delights, such as Hojicha Panna Cotta, ice cream flavors, pudding, and taiyaki, showcase a delightful range of textures and flavors from Japanese cuisine.

Hojicha Panna Cotta

Fusing Italian creaminess with Japanese Hojicha's nutty, roasted flavor. Smooth texture and toasty taste create a delightful treat.

  

 

Ice cream

Assorted frozen desserts in flavors like vanilla, chocolate, green tea matcha, and unique options such as black sesame or yuzu. Known for creamy texture and distinct flavors.

 

Pudding

Silky smooth custard akin to crème caramel. Flavored with vanilla, caramel, or matcha. Renowned for delicate sweetness and velvety consistency.

  

 

 

Taiyaki

Fish-shaped cake with sweet fillings like red bean paste, custard, chocolate, or sweet potato. Crispy outside, fluffy inside—a delightful dessert choice for restaurant menus.
